wikileaks.ch

The Pirate Party Switzerland as holder of this domain has been informed by the Swiss Registry Switch about a malware served on Wikileaks and threatend with delisting of this domain. The Wikileaks team has been contacted but has not yet reacted to this situation. For more information, please consult our press release.

Continue to wikileaks.org at your own risk.

Visit the Pirate Party site in German or French.

Wikileaks staff is invited to contact Mr. Guillaume Saouli @gsaouli to discuss the issue.
